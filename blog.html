    30-Minute Gym Workout

    Your running journey is an exhilarating experience, but to truly elevate your performance and safeguard against injuries, the importance of cross training cannot be overstated. Runners often have questions like: how to start running again?

    A 30-minute gym workout at FlexFusion is perfect for those short on time but committed to fitness. ...Focus on high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to maximize calorie burn and enhance cardiovascular health.
